Arduino Project

Description: I created a fully functional single-board microcontroller program.

Process and Planning: First I did a tutorial on how to operate the arduino kit. Then I look at the other tutorials that was offered. I planned to choose the traffic lights one because it was simply and easy to replicate. I decided to add an LED light to the program due to the requirement of the project of adding something different from the tutorial.

What I learned: Programming a single-board is diffcult then what it seems. Pay attention on how you place different components on the board because it can affect how it's suppose to operate.

What I could've done differently: Choose a different tutorial to replicate becuase the tutorial I did was kind of easy and less time consuming. I wanted a challenge, something that would rack my brain with.
